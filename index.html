NASA Space Apps?

NASA Space Apps is an annual international hackathon that brings together global participants to tackle challenges in space exploration and Earth science. Organized by NASA, it encourages teamwork, creativity, and innovation using NASA's open data. This event unites experts and enthusiasts from diverse backgrounds, making it a hub for advancing science and technology. Join us in Thrissur to be part of this global movement with a unique local touch.

The NASA International Space Apps Challenge is a hackathon for coders, scientists, designers, storytellers, makers, technologists, and innovators around the world to come together and use open data from NASA and its Space Agency Partners to create solutions to challenges we face on Earth and in space.

Earth's Largest Local Event

NASA Space Apps '23, the Earth's largest local event, was successfully hosted at Jyothi Engineering College in Thrissur, showcasing innovative solutions for space apps challenges.
